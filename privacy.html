Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

DropSnooze is built privacy-first. It collects no personal data, sends none of your content off your Mac, and works fully offline.

What we collect

Nothing. DropSnooze has no account, no sign-in, and no analytics or tracking. The developer never receives any information about you or how you use the app.

Your content stays on your Mac

Everything you snooze — files, links, notes, and images — is stored locally on your Mac. Files you keep as references are accessed through macOS security-scoped bookmarks; their contents are never copied to us or to any server. DropSnooze makes no network requests to transmit your content.

In-app purchase

DropSnooze offers a one-time purchase to unlock unlimited use, processed by Apple's App Store. The transaction is handled entirely by Apple — we never see your payment details, and none of your content is transmitted as part of a purchase. Apple may process purchase data under its own Apple Privacy Policy.

No third parties

DropSnooze contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Notifications

DropSnooze uses local notifications, scheduled and delivered entirely on your Mac, to resurface your snoozed items at the time you chose. Nothing about these notifications leaves your device.

Children's privacy

DropSnooze does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
