Support & FAQ

DropSnooze gives "later" a button: drag anything onto the menu bar, pick a time — it comes back and opens.

Questions, bugs, or ideas? Email sllbailuo@outlook.com — I read every message. (Settings → "Send Feedback" fills this in for you, with your app and macOS version.)

Where does DropSnooze live?

In the menu bar (top-right of your screen), not the Dock. Drag any file, link, note, or image onto the boomerang icon, hold for a moment, and the time wheel springs open.

How is it different from shelf apps like Yoink or Dropover?

Those hold things while you move them around. DropSnooze answers a different question — when should this come back? Shelves hold; DropSnooze brings things back at the time you picked, and opens them.

Why not just use Reminders or a to-do app?

Turning "deal with this later" into a task takes about six steps. DropSnooze is one gesture — and at the end you get the file or page itself reopened, not a line of text about it.

Does it copy or move my files?

No. Files stay exactly where they are. DropSnooze remembers them with security-scoped bookmarks and reopens the original in place. Deleting an item in DropSnooze never deletes your file — stashed copies go to the Trash, recoverable.

Does it connect to the internet?

The app makes zero network requests — there is no networking code in it. No account, no sync, no analytics. The App Store privacy label is "Data Not Collected". See the privacy policy.

How much does it cost?

Free to download and try — stash 5 items before it asks you anything. Unlimited is a one-time $7.99 purchase. No subscription, ever.

Why isn't it available in the EU or mainland China?

As a solo developer I currently don't meet the EU Digital Services Act "trader" disclosure requirements (publishing a personal address and phone number). Mainland China requires separate arrangements. I may revisit both later.

System requirements

macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later. Universal — Apple silicon and Intel.

How do refunds work?

Purchases are handled by Apple. Request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com — Apple reviews requests directly.

简体中文

DropSnooze 给"稍后处理"一个按钮：把任何文件、链接、文字或图片拖到菜单栏，挑个时间——到点它自己回来，并打开。有问题发邮件到 sllbailuo@outlook.com，每封都会看（设置里的「发送反馈」会帮你带上版本信息）。

DropSnooze 在哪里？

在菜单栏右上角（不在 Dock）。把东西拖到回旋镖图标上悬停一下，时间盘就会弹出。

和 Yoink、Dropover 这类搁置架有什么区别？

搁置架解决"东西先放哪"，DropSnooze 解决"什么时候回来"——到点它会自己回来并打开原文件，这是搁置架没有的。

会复制或移动我的文件吗？

不会。文件原地不动，通过 macOS 安全作用域书签原位重新打开；在 DropSnooze 里删除条目永远不会删除你的文件（收纳副本进废纸篓、可恢复）。

它联网吗？

零网络请求——App 里根本没有联网代码。无账号、无同步、无统计，App Store 隐私标签是「未收集数据」。详见隐私政策。

多少钱？

免费下载试用（可存入 5 次），一次性 $7.99 解锁无限，永远没有订阅。

为什么欧盟和中国大陆买不到？

独立开发者暂未满足欧盟 DSA 的经营者公示要求（需公开个人住址电话）；中国大陆需要另行安排。以后可能开放。

系统要求

macOS 14 (Sonoma) 及以上，Apple 芯片与 Intel 通用。

怎么退款？

App Store 的退款由 Apple 处理：在 reportaproblem.apple.com 申请，Apple 直接审核。
