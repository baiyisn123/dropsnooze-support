Questions, answered

How is it different from shelf apps like Yoink or Dropover?

Shelves hold things while you move them around. DropSnooze answers a different question — when should this come back? — and opens it at that time.

Does it copy or move my files?

No. Files stay where they are; security-scoped bookmarks reopen the original in place. Deleting in DropSnooze never deletes your file.

Does it connect to the internet?

The app makes zero network requests — no account, no analytics. The App Store privacy label is "Data Not Collected". See the privacy policy.

What does it cost?

Free to try — stash 5 items. Unlimited is a one-time $7.99 purchase. No subscription, ever.
