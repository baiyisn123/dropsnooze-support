## FAQ

**How does DropSnooze work?**
Drag any file, link, note, or image onto the menu-bar icon, hold for a moment, and a time wheel appears. Drop it on a time (Tonight, Tomorrow, 1 hour…). It disappears, and at that time it comes back — and opens — automatically.

**Where do my files go? Are they copied?**
By default DropSnooze does **not** copy your files — it remembers where they are (using a secure bookmark) and reopens the original in place. You can switch to "stash" mode in **Settings → Drop handling**, which moves a copy into DropSnooze's own storage.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. DropSnooze is 100% offline — it makes no network requests, has no account, and collects nothing. Everything stays on your Mac.

**How much does it cost?**
DropSnooze is free to try — you can snooze your first **5 items** free. After that, a single **one-time purchase** unlocks unlimited snoozing, forever. No subscription. Anything you've already saved stays put.

**How do I unlock or restore my purchase?**
**Settings → Purchase** to unlock. If you've bought it before (e.g., on another Mac), use **Settings → Restore Purchase**.

**A reminder didn't appear.**
DropSnooze needs notification permission to alert you when an item is due. If you denied it, enable it in **System Settings → Notifications → DropSnooze**. (Items still come back to your inbox even without notifications.)

**There's no Dock icon.**
That's intended — DropSnooze lives in the **menu bar**, not the Dock. Click the menu-bar icon to open it.

**How do I see everything I've parked?**
Click the menu-bar icon — the **Inbox** and **Scheduled** tabs show everything, on a timeline.
